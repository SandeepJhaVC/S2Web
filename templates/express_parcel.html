EXPRESS PARCEL

Our express parcel delivery network services over 18,000 pin codes in India. We handle consignments of up to 10 kilograms with same-day and next-day capabilities and 48-96 hour deliveries for long-distance orders. Additionally, we offer heavy goods service comprising the warehousing and delivery of heavy goods, such as large electrical goods, furniture, and sports equipment, supported by our extensive pan-India network and PTL services. We also extend our express parcel services for domestic shipping through s2logistics, delivering directly to individual consumers from the comfort of their homes.
